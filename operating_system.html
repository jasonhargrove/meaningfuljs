OS_like_OS

Requirements

To run OS_like_OS on your computer you need:
  • 2 GHz or faster processor
  • 1 GB RAM
  • 200 MB available hard disk space

Installation

To install OS_like_OS on your computer:
  1. Download the bootable image from our site
  2. Write it to a USB drive or an optical disk
  3. Boot your computer with it
  4. Follow instructions on the screen
